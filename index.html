The Field Guide of Fungi Crafts

Welcome, friends! Fungi offer artists many opportunities for enriching their practice. As someone who is constantly creating, you are probably also eager to find ways to experiment. The purpose of this guide is to empower you to use fungi in as many ways as you can dream up. This guide is the work of many people. Please consider submitting your experiences, corrections, and additions.
